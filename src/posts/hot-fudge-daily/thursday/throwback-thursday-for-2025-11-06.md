Let's turn back the clock and see what the tech world was buzzing about on this day in years past.

### 5 Years Ago: [November 6, 2020](https://www.techmeme.com/201106/h2355)

Netflix invented [a TV station](https://www.techmeme.com/201106/p7#a201106p7)... then went all-in on offering [a variety of linear and live programming](https://www.techmeme.com/250804/p15#a250804p15).

### 10 Years Ago: [November 6, 2015](https://www.techmeme.com/151106/h2355)

Verizon determined [hyperscale is expensive](https://www.techmeme.com/151106/p15#a151106p15)... then moved to AWS [8 years later](https://www.techmeme.com/180515/p27#a180515p27).

### 15 Years Ago: [November 6, 2010](https://www.techmeme.com/101106/h2355)

Amazon acquired [Quidsi](https://www.techmeme.com/101106/h2355)... then shut it down [7 years later](https://www.techmeme.com/170329/p22#a170329p22).
