### 04:40 PM

Email delivery is sometimes not good.

Emails like hotel confirmation and cancellation notices are useful.

I stay in Marriott properties or Hilton depending on the best prices. I've lived years of my life in each. So, it can be a coin flip.

Welp...

At some point in mid Februrary 2026, `res-marriott.com` domain ran afoul of whatever Google Workspace decided was not strict enough SPF, DKIM, DMARC, or whatever passes for the growing list of being this tall to ride that delivery ride for `reservations@res-marriott.com` .

At the minimum... these... but I am guessing something else is b0rked:

  * SPF and DKIM authentication
  * From: header alignment
  * DMARC authentication
  * Encryption
  * User-reported spam rate
  * DNS records
  * One-click unsubscribe
  * Honor unsubscribe



So, I'm trading emails with Bonvoy support as a Lifetime Titanium Elite member so that I can get a confirmation email by hunting down Message ID logs since February 2026.

Notably, other less useful marketing materials from `marriott.com` and similar communications are just fine. For now?
