### 11:24 PM

A [#mastodonadmin](https://cuthrell.com/tags/mastodonadmin) workaround

<code>  
$ git diff -- app/controllers/auth/sessions_controller.rb

def home_paths(resource)  
\- paths = [about_path, '/explore']  
\+ paths = [about_path, '/about']

paths << short_account_path(username: resource.account) if single_user_mode? && resource.is_a?(User)  
</code>

So, now when someone on the interwebs visits cuthrell.com it sends them to "about" instead of the default which was set to "explore" which is of little use for a single-user instance.

### 06:43 PM

💡 [#notes](https://cuthrell.com/tags/notes) on XYZ is dead

Bacronym D.E.A.D. as a progression with camelcase to indicate where a technology is… and capitalization to indicate the end of a cycle from dead to DEAD.

developed becomes Deployed

edge case becomes Enterprise

advisable becomes Alternatives

deprecated becomes Decommissioned

[#wardleymap](https://cuthrell.com/tags/wardleymap) into ibles and ables of possible, permissible, sustainable, repeatable, and advisable — the implication being sometimes things are D.E.a.d. on arrival in most Enterpise businesses.
