I remember my last glass of wine. I remember my last shot. I remember my last cocktail. I remember my last beer.

I miss following [#sxsw](https://cuthrell.com/tags/sxsw) updates but then again I miss [#sxsw](https://cuthrell.com/tags/sxsw) in general.
