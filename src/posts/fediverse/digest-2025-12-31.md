## Dec 31

### 12:58 AM

{% raw %}
Oh no... it's the dreaded year in review post.

I've been writing daily for years and can't recall the last time I took a break. I've been late to send or had a few technical hiccups but otherwise... typing and sending.

This year I stopped attempting LinkedIn Newsletters. I decided POSSE, n8n, and python scripts would be my approach to sharing as LinkedIn posts instead with options for Fediverse (originally Mastodon now GoToSocial) and Bluesky.

My LinkedIn traffic was lower this year with only 179,507 Impressions, 4,713 Engagements, and 3,253 Profile Viewers. I set a mini-goal of reaching 6,000 followers by the end of 2025 and as of this email I am sitting at around 5,985.

Oddly enough, my three most popular posts on LinkedIn in 2025 were one mistakenly late published article... and two copy/paste from my actual newsletter / blog.

When I look at the entire year of posts, my reach spikes significantly when I am a "live reporter" for industry events like Tech Field Day, All Things AI, CISE, SXSW, etc.

I traveled a lot this year and reached Diamond levels on Delta, Hilton, and Marriott. So, if that travel level continues in 2026, perhaps I'll be seeing more spikes in my reach.

As for writing in 2026, I might slow it down a bit. My formulaic approach for Hot Fudge Daily lasted for well over a year but I missed Fudge Factor nerdy stuff.

HNY - Be safe - Be well
{% endraw %}
