Rules of the game:  
\- Grab the nearest book.  
\- Turn to page 42  
\- Find the 2nd sentence  
\- Post the sentence in a toot with the hashtag & write the rules as a comment to it  
\- Don't look for your favourite, coolest or wittiest book. Go for the closest.
