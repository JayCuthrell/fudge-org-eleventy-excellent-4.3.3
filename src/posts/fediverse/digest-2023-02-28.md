The tendency of YouTube and other social platforms to overwhelmingly weight current IP address ranges as a basis for recommendations is pretty much the mélange of cynical, banal, clichéd, and awful as you might expect.

As CGNAT, device anonymization, private relay / proxy becomes more commonly available, IP address weighting will become a historical footnote in bad design choices by poorly informed marketing teams.
