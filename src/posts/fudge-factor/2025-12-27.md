### 🚀🛰️👔 Leadership Updates

- 🇺🇸🏛️ United Launch Alliance (ULA) named [John Elbon](https://www.govconwire.com/articles/ula-john-elbon-interim-ceo-tory-bruno-departure) as interim CEO following [Tony Bruno's departure](https://newsroom.ulalaunch.com/releases/statement-from-robert-lightfoot-and-kay-sears) from the ~20 year old joint venture between Lockheed Martin and Boeing
- 🇺🇸🏛️ Sidus Space announced that [Carol Craig](https://www.linkedin.com/in/unconventionalceo/), CEO, has been [appointed to the Canaveral Port Authority Board of Commissioners](https://investors.sidusspace.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/266/sidus-space-ceo-carol-craig-appointed-to-canaveral-port)
