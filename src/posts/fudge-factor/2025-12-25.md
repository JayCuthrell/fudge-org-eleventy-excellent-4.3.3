### Orbital Compute Pivot

I believe [Google's Project Suncatcher](https://fudge.org/archive/fudge-factor-returns/) got the [attention](https://fudge.org/archive/orbital-edge-space-datacenters-era/) of NVIDIA and [lit a fire](https://fudge.org/archive/orbital-compute-baseline/). The only thing more premium than a XPU on Earth is compute engineered for aerospace, orbital space, cislunar space, an outer space.

For example, Starcloud-2 will utilize NVIDIA Blackwell H100's and B200's for [in-orbit processing of Capella Space's SAR (synthetic-aperture radar) satellite constellation data](https://www.geekwire.com/2025/starcloud-power-training-ai-space/). The real question will be what Starcloud-3 will utilize.
