### üìö A Growing List of of Space Value Chain (SVC) Companies

I've been thinking about the growing list of space value chain (SVC) companies. But how to visualize that market landscape?

For now, I've decide on [https://github.com/cncf/landscape2](https://github.com/cncf/landscape2) and checked in a mostly working version: [https://github.com/JayCuthrell/svc.fudge.org](https://github.com/JayCuthrell/svc.fudge.org)

The result? [Space Value Chain (SVC) Landscape](https://svc.fudge.org) ü§ì The GitHub Actions build process even generates a [landscape.png](https://svc.fudge.org/docs/landscape.png) and [landscape.pdf](https://svc.fudge.org/docs/landscape.pdf). Pull requests [welcomed](https://github.com/JayCuthrell/svc.fudge.org/pulls)!
